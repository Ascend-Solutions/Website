VIP Payroll

VIP Payroll is an interactive payroll system that runs in the Microsoft Windows Environment. It enables you to set up a database containing pay details of the employees in your organization. From this information the system generates several statutory payroll reports such as the payroll summary, pension contributions, year-end tax returns and, of course, pay slips.

There is an edition of VIP Payroll written specifically for the Kenyan market. It will therefore satisfy your requirements with respect to currency, statutory deductions and banking. A separate edition for Uganda and other African countries such as Tanzania, South Africa etc. are also available. Currently, over 50,000 organizations in Africa including Kenya are using the VIP Payroll.
